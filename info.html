How can you protect yourself from Covid?

  • Always wear a mask when outside or meeting with other people!
  • Disinfect surfaces around your home and work!
  • Wash your hands for at least 20 seconds!
  • Cover your mouth when you sneeze or cough!
  • Avoid close contact with sick people, and when you're sick, maintain distance at least 6 feet!
  • Prevent touching something contaminated with germs, and then to eyes, nose, or mouth!
